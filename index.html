How to Play TypeRacer
  1. Select your desired difficulty level.
  2. Click Start to begin the test.
  3. Type the sample text as accurately and quickly as possible in the input area.
  4. Click Stop when you finish typing.
  5. Your results (Level, Time, WPM) will be displayed.
  6. Click Retry to try again at the same difficulty.
